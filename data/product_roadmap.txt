BRIGHTWAVE TECHNOLOGIES INC.
PRODUCT ROADMAP — FISCAL YEAR 2026
Document Owner: Product Management Team

1. VISION

In 2026, Brightwave aims to become the leading mid-market supply chain analytics platform by deepening predictive capabilities, expanding integration coverage, and improving self-serve onboarding to reduce time-to-value for new customers from 6 weeks to under 2 weeks.

2. Q1 GOALS (JANUARY - MARCH)

- Launch self-serve onboarding flow, removing the requirement for a dedicated implementation consultant for Starter and Professional tier customers.
- Ship native integrations with SAP and NetSuite, the two most requested ERP systems from customer feedback.
- Improve dashboard load times by 40% through query optimization and caching improvements.
- Priority: High. These items directly address the top 3 churn reasons identified in Q4 2025 customer exit interviews.

3. Q2 GOALS (APRIL - JUNE)

- Release the Inventory Optimization module, using demand forecasting models to recommend reorder points and safety stock levels.
- Introduce role-based dashboards so operations, finance, and executive users see tailored views of the same underlying data.
- Expand webhook and API capabilities to support real-time event subscriptions for enterprise customers.
- Priority: High for Inventory Optimization (flagship feature for the year), Medium for role-based dashboards and webhook expansion.

4. Q3 GOALS (JULY - SEPTEMBER)

- Launch multi-region data residency options (EU and APAC) to unblock enterprise deals with data sovereignty requirements.
- Ship anomaly detection alerts for shipment delays and demand spikes, delivered via Slack, email, and in-app notifications.
- Complete SOC 2 Type II certification to support enterprise procurement requirements.
- Priority: High for SOC 2 certification (blocking multiple enterprise deals currently in the pipeline), Medium for data residency and anomaly detection.

5. Q4 GOALS (OCTOBER - DECEMBER)

- Launch the Partner Marketplace, allowing third-party logistics providers to publish integrations directly within the platform.
- Introduce AI-assisted report generation, letting users generate natural-language summaries of analytics dashboards.
- Conduct a full pricing and packaging review based on a year of usage data, with proposed changes targeted for early 2027.
- Priority: Medium for Partner Marketplace and AI-assisted reporting, High for the pricing review given its downstream revenue impact.

6. FEATURE PRIORITIES SUMMARY

Overall prioritization for FY2026, ranked by expected business impact:
1. SOC 2 Type II certification — unblocks approximately $2.4M in stalled enterprise pipeline.
2. Inventory Optimization module — flagship differentiator versus competitors, expected to drive Professional-to-Enterprise upgrades.
3. Self-serve onboarding — directly reduces time-to-value and Q1-identified churn risk.
4. ERP integrations (SAP, NetSuite) — removes the most common blocker cited in lost deals.
5. Multi-region data residency — required for EU and APAC expansion.
6. Partner Marketplace and AI-assisted reporting — longer-term differentiation, lower urgency for FY2026.

Roadmap items are subject to reprioritization at each quarterly business review based on sales pipeline shifts and customer feedback trends.
